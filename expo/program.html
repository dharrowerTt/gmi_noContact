Methane Expo 2013 Comprehensive Program

Methane Expo 2013 Comprehensive Program Will Feature:

Five Sector-Specific Conference Tracks:
Key methane abatement, recovery and use technologies and policy issues as well as important barriers to project development and how to overcome them in each of the GMI sectors: agriculture, coal mines, municipal solid waste, municipal wastewater, and oil and natural gas systems.

High-Level Plenary Sessions:
Leaders from government, industry, and multi-lateral development banks will discuss current efforts to overcome barriers to project development around the globe.

Methane Marketplace:
Methane recovery and use projects ripe for immediate financing or implementation, as well as technology providers showcasing the latest products and services.

Partnership Working Meetings:
Government and industry discussions on how GMI can effectively promote methane abatement, recovery and use projects and activities around the world.
