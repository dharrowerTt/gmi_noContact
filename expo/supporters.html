BC Bioenergy Network

The BC Bioenergy Network is a not for profit organization that accelerates the development of bioenergy in British Columbia through supporting pilot and full scale demonstration projects, capacity building, and education and advocacy activities. A number of supported waste to energy projects are demonstrating excellent methane reduction results that can be replicated both in BC and in overseas markets.

World Bank-Global Gas Flaring Reduction Partnership

The World Bank-led Global Gas Flaring Reduction partnership supports the efforts of oil producing countries and companies to increase the use of associated natural gas and thus reduce flaring and venting, which wastes valuable resources and damages the environment.

Through public-private collaboration GGFR partners are contributing towards improving energy efficiency, reducing GHG emissions from flaring, and expanding access to electricity.
