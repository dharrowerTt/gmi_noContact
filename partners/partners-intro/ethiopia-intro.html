Ethiopia

Based on data in EPA's Global Anthropogenic Emissions of Non-CO2 Greenhouse Gases report, in 2010, Ethiopia's estimated anthropogenic methane emissions ranked 23rd in the world. While livestock is the country's largest source of methane emissions, approximately 14 percent of its anthropogenic methane emissions—8.87 MMTCO2E—come from agriculture (manure management), municipal solid waste, and wastewater.
