Mongolia

Based on data in EPA's Global Anthropogenic Emissions of Non—CO2 Greenhouse Gases report, in 2005, Mongolia's estimated anthropogenic methane emissions ranked 68th in the world. Approximately 5 percent of its anthropogenic methane emissions—390,000 MTCO2E—come from agriculture (manure management), coal mines, and municipal solid waste.
