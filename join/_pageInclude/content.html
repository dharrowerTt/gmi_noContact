Join A Subcommittee

GMI’s Technical Subcommittees are organized by the three main sources of methane emissions globally: Oil & Gas, Biogas, and Coal Mines. These three Subcommittees support methane emissions reduction by providing collaboration opportunities, capacity-building support, and technical assistance to remove technical and non-technical barriers to methane mitigation project development. The Subcommittees also facilitate investment and financing opportunities and other cooperative activities and projects to advance the abatement, recovery, and use of methane.

Who Can Participate?

Each Subcommittee includes representatives from interested GMI Partner Countries, non-governmental organizations, and Project Network members. Each Partner may appoint up to three members to each Subcommittee.

Project Network members or interested organizations, such as Strategic Partners, may serve as official delegates or participate informally in a Subcommittee by consent of that Subcommittee.

Ready to Join?

If you are a GMI Partner Country, nominate a delegate by sending an email to secretariat@globalmethane.org. You must have a government email address to nominate a Partner Country delegate. Please include the nominee’s name, email address, organization, and title in your email.

If you are a Project Network Member, send an email to secretariat@globalmethane.org to either nominate an official delegate or request to participate informally in Subcommittee meetings. Please include the nominee’s name, email address, organization, and title in your email.

If you are not yet affiliated with GMI, please review our Join GMI page to get involved.
