Call for Presentations

Thank you to all of the Partners and Project Network members that submitted presentation abstracts for potential inclusion in the Expo's technical and policy session program. Abstract topics included methane mitigation from agricultural sources, coal mines, landfills, and oil and natural gas systems, as well as cross-cutting issues such as financing, emission inventories, and policy or regulatory frameworks.

All presentations are available on our Proceedings page.
