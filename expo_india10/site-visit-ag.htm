Agriculture Site Tour

Attendees of the Partnership Expo interested in the agriculture sector had a special opportunity on 2 March to tour the pilot level project demonstration of Biogas enrichment and bottling technology developed by the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi at Shri Krishna Gaushala, Karera village in Ghaziabad.
