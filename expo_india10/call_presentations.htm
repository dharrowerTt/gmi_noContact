Call for Presentations

The Methane to Markets Partnership is holding its 2nd Methane to Markets Partnership Expo on 2-5 March 2010, in New Delhi, India. We invite your potential participation in the workshop and policy session component of the Expo by submitting a one-page presentation abstract.

Abstracts may cover any topic related to methane mitigation from the following general subject areas:

  • Agricultural Sources
  • Coal Mining
  • Landfills
  • Oil and Natural Gas
  • Cross-Cutting Issues

Please Note: Presentations should be about the deployment of commercially proven, 3rd-party verified technologies, not research and development nor marketing presentations.

Submissions are due by: 15 August 2009

Abstracts will be reviewed by the Methane to Markets Administrative Support Group (ASG) and the Sector Subcommittees. We will provide notification of acceptance or denial by 1 December 2009.

For a complete list of potential topics and submission requirements please download the complete Call for Presentations (PDF, 2 pp., 24KB).
