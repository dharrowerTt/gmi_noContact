Eco Securities: EcoSecurities is one of the world's leading companies in the business of originating, developing and trading carbon credits. The last 10 years has seen EcoSecurities involved in the development of many of the global carbon market's most important milestones, including developing the world's first Clean Development Mechanism (CDM) project to be registered under the Kyoto Protocol, and the first to be issued with carbon credits. Today, the company is working on 374 projects in 36 countries using 18 different technologies, with the potential to generate more than 163 million carbon credits. In 2006, EcoSecurities was voted 'Best CDM/JI Project Developer' by both Point Carbon and Environmental Finance magazine.
