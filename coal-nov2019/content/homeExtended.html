About our Meeting Partner

The UNECE Group of Experts on Coal Mine Methane promotes mine safety and sustainability practices. The Group’s activities focus on climate change mitigation and best practices to reduce CMM emissions and abandoned mine methane (AMM) drainage. The 14th session of the Group of Experts will include:

  • A review of the accomplishments achieved under the 2018-2019 work plan,
  • Discussion of the work plan for 2020-2021,
  • Exploration of research needs in the field of CMM, and
  • Special focus on issues faced by women resulting from the gradual phase-out of coal in many countries.
