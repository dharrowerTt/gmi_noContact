Please Join Us

The Global Methane Initiative’s (GMI) Steering Committee will convene for its annual in-person meeting on 18-19 November 2019 in Paris, France. This year, the 2-day meeting will take place at the United Nations Environment Programme, UNESCO headquarters annex, hosted by the Climate and Clean Air Coalition (CCAC) secretariat.

The Steering Committee meeting is scheduled to take place on Monday, 18 November, from 10:00-17:30 and on Tuesday, 19 November, from 10:00-15:45.

The address for the meeting is:

UNESCO Headquarters Annex
Miollis Building
1 Rue Miollis
75732 Paris Cedex 15
France

The GMI Steering Committee meeting will be held in the Ground Floor Meeting Room in Building 7 (the UNEP offices) on the smaller of the two UNESCO sites at 1 Rue Miollis, not the main Fontenoy site. Google maps link.

Please allow 15 to 30 minutes for security check-in. Zeena Hilton (+33 6 40 63 64 97) will be at security reception with your badges. Please bring your passport.

The purpose of the Steering Committee meeting is for participants to get an update on the exciting developments related to the Global Methane Challenge and get involved in planning for the 2020 Global Methane Forum to be held in Geneva in March 2020. The Steering Committee will also begin to discuss the future of the GMI, as the current charter ends in April 2021. This dialogue will be continued at future Steering Committee meetings, including during the Global Methane Forum.

We look forward to seeing you in Paris.
