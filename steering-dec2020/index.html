GMI Steering Committee Meeting

Steering Committee Meeting Overview

The Global Methane Initiative’s (GMI) Steering Committee will convene for a meeting on Wednesday, 2 December and Thursday, 3 December 2020 during 2-to 3-hour sessions each day. This year, the annual meeting will take place virtually using the Microsoft Teams platform. We are excited about the opportunity to engage with you “in person” for an interactive discussion.

The purpose of the Steering Committee meeting is to review feedback provided by the Executive Task Force and make decisions regarding key questions about the future of GMI. Discussion topics will include:

  • Prioritizing the objectives of GMI
  • Considering options to extend the GMI charter
  • Leveraging strategic partnerships
  • Supporting the UN International Year (or Decade) of Methane Management
  • Leveraging the Global Methane Challenge
  • Assessing GMI’s governance structure

Co-Chairs of the GMI Biogas, Coal, and Oil & Gas Subcommittees are invited to provide updates about sector-specific accomplishments and plans for 2021 and participate in the discussions about the future of GMI.



For more information contact us at secretariat@globalmethane.org.
