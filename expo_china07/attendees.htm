Attendees

Who Should Attend?

The Expo is open to anyone interested in learning about the most recent developments in methane recovery and use project opportunities and technologies. The Expo Agenda will benefit:

  • Project Developers and Financiers
  • Project Developers and Financiers
  • Policymakers and Government Representatives
  • Manufacturers and Vendors
  • Industry Representatives
  • International Consultants

Why Attend?

The Expo's comprehensive program, forums, trade show, and networking opportunities will give attendees opportunities to:

  • Identify and explore project opportunities with industry partners.
  • Learn about international financing and projects seeking development partners.
  • Join high-level national delegations and participate in policy discussions.
  • Help advance country-based projects.
  • Participate in an international marketplace with a methane focus.
  • Demonstrate and market the latest methane recovery and use technologies and services.
  • Interact and discuss projects with technology vendors and developers.
  • Learn about options for implementing methane capture and use projects.
