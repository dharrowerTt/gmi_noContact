Become a sponsor of the 2007 Methane to Markets Partnership Expo and take advantage of the largest international gathering dedicated to methane recovery and use projects and technologies.

Why sponsor? The expo will provide excellent opportunities to:

  • Interact with high-level government officials and policymakers.
  • Showcase technologies and projects to developers and financiers.
  • Market your products and services to prospective customers from all over the world.
  • Network with other vendors and industry representatives.
  • Gain full access to the Expo's comprehensive program, including all training sessions, technical tours, and panel presentations.

Sponsorship Application

There are two types of sponsorships available. You can be the sole-sponsor of a specific event or you can sponsor at a particular level and receive benefits for each level, including exhibit space. For complete details about all the sponsorship opportunities available, please download the Sponsorship Application Form.

Please fax completed forms to the Administrative Support Group at +1-781-674-2906.
