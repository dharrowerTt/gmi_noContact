Improvement of public awareness

  • Share information on oil and gas sector specific methane emissions

Mitigation Activities

  • Flaring in place of venting for both offshore and onshore gas wells

  • Production abatement options
  • Processing abatement options
  • Transmission abatement options
  • Distribution abatement options

Policy
