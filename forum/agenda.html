Global Methane Forum Agenda

Please take a moment to fill out a short, 5-minute evaluation of the Global Methane Forum. Your feedback will help determine how GMI plans its future meetings.

The Global Methane Initiative (GMI) and Climate and Clean Air Coalition (CCAC) have developed a preliminary agenda for the Forum. Below is an overview of events scheduled for the full week. The detailed Forum agenda can be downloaded here: Preliminary Global Methane Forum Agenda (PDF, 16pp, 245KB).
