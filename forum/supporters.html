Ful-Vane™ compressors have over 85 years of proven experience. GHGSat offers methane detection, measurement and monitoring services, using its innovative satellite technology. GHGSat's satellite can measure methane emissions from targeted industrial facilities, with high precision and at lower cost than comparable alternatives. Since the same satellite can measure any facility in the world, GHGSat can provide consistent, reliable measurements across jurisdictions.
