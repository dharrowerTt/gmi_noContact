Trido Industries: Methane Reduction through Electrification of Pneumatic Pumps and Devices 

Trido Industries is focused on zero-vent technologies powered by small solar panels and batteries. They have developed solar-powered chemical injection pumps to replace pneumatic chemical and circulation pumps at oil and gas facilities in Canada and the United States. Their zero-vent pumps use a low amount of power and high torque to handle extreme pressures of 10,000 pounds per square inch or more.

Trido Industries’ technology has replaced 3,500 pumps. Based on tracking the stroke count on the units, the pumps reduce approximately 385,000 tons of carbon dioxide equivalent (CO2e) per year. Trido Industries estimates that an additional 110,000 tons of CO2e could be reduced each year by implementing their technology.
