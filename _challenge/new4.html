Methane Reductions

Some reports say that as much as 51% of fugitive emissions from the oil and gas industry are a result of leaking valves. In a study done by the University of Colorado they found that 13 million metric tons of methane is leaked into the atmosphere from oil and gas production. This equates to roughly 325 MMTCO2e with about 166 MMTCO2e contributed by leaking valves. MagDrive Solutions, Inc. hopes to combat this problem with our Leak-free valve technology.
