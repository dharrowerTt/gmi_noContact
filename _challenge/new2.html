Measuring Effectiveness

Process Ecology generates annual reports (internal) that highlight the year to year reductions in emissions from the fleet we manage. With currently enacted methane regulations we expect to see continued reductions in the coming years but it is difficult to estimate what the rate of change would be.

Expected Outcomes

As part of our service we gather data and measurements every year on every plant that is operating for our client-base (currently over 2,000 facilities). The detailed engineering calculations we use coupled with regular sampling and analysis of gas compositions enable us to track year to year the performance of these operations.

Methane Reductions

The ultimate goal has been estimated as approximately 1MT CO2 eq/yr from these actions. Assuming a 40% success rate then it would be equivalent to 400,000 T CO2e/yr
