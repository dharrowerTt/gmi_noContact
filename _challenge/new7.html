UNIPAK TRADING INC, Capital Markets Financing of BioGas Production from Waste

We have targeted California, USA and New York State, USA as the starting geographies. However, we have also engaged development the same concept in Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, North Africa and Pakistan.

The action is mainly domestic (USA) to prove the viability of this approach to portfolio finance. Eventually, we will advance this globally starting with Morocco, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and Pakistan.

Partners: We are working with multiple stakeholders (BioGas plant machinery producers; feed stock such as waste water, municipal waste, farm waste producers; project developers; finance providers including asset managers, government finance institutions, carbon finance firms; gas off takers such as utility companies, natural gas vehicle fueling stations; service providers for fund management, impact measurement etc.).

Measuring Effectiveness

Completion of the work stream components (that are mentioned above) is the definition of success by work stream. Success of our effort over all is the issuance of the capital markets instrument backed by the underlying pool of BioGas projects (that achieve the emissions reductions, sustainable impact).

Expected Outcomes

Success is our capability to create these multiple BioGas projects and pool them into capital markets traded securities that asset management investors can purchase. Progress in this direction and the incremental steps that get us closer and closer to this goal are the measures we are tracking. For e.g., deliverable items include securing physical project site, securing feed stock, securing financing, securing EPC contractors, securing off take agreements etc.

Methane Reductions

We eventually aim to reduce carbon emissions from 100Thousand tons per day of waste. That will be avoiding emissions of 35Million tons of CO2 (35MMTCO2e) per annum once all the projects are implemented. On a project by project basis, that equates to 0.2 MMTCO2e to 0.5 MMTCO2e
