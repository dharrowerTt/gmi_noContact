UNIPAK Trading Inc.: Financing Biogas Projects 

UNIPAK Trading Inc. is working to develop securities that can be traded in capital markets and purchased by institutional asset management investors to satisfy their sustainable development and responsible, low-carbon investment mandates. UNIPAK is working with multiple stakeholders to create a market instrument backed by an underlying pool of biogas projects. Development activities are primarily focused in California, the Northeastern United States, and across Turkey. Investment risks are mitigated with carbon values, incentives, and diverse downstream biogas uses (e.g., combined heat power, natural gas vehicle fueling, and pipeline injection). Their work has established the global viability of financing biogas projects as portfolios of a distinct asset class. UNIPAK is working with many stakeholders, including:

  • Biogas plant machinery producers
  • Feedstock producers (wastewater, municipal waste, and farm waste)
  • Project developers
  • Finance providers (asset managers, government finance institutions, and carbon finance firms)
  • Biogas end users (utility companies, natural gas vehicle fueling stations, on-site power users)
  • Providers of other services, such as fund management, impact measurement, etc.

Through the development of the capital market securities, UNIPAK aims to help launch projects that reduce carbon emissions from 100,000 tons per day of waste, which could result in avoiding approximately 35 MMTCO2e per year once all the projects are implemented.
