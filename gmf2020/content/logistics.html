Onsite Logistics

Entry Badges

UN entrance badges are obtained at the Pass and Identification Unit of the UNOG Security and Safety Section, located at the Pregny Gate, 14, Avenue de la Paix, opposite the Red Cross Building.

  • Bring your ID or passport to obtain your entrance badge.
  • Refer to “Global Methane Forum 2020, 23-27 March 2020” when asking for your entry badge.
  • Allow approximately 40 minutes to obtain your UN Entrance Badge and reach the meeting room. The Security Office opens at 08.00 hours and closes at 17.00 hours.

Public Transportation

The Palais des Nations is served by several tram and bus lines: Buses 8, 28, F, V and Z serve Pregny Gate (Appia stop).

  • Tram 15, as well as buses 5, 8, 11, 22, 28, V, F and Z, serve the Place des Nations.
  • Buses 5 and 28 serve Geneva Airport from the Place des Nations.
Additional information is available at TPG Genève.

Taxis

A taxi stand is located on Route de Pregny near the Pregny Gate. Taxis are usually available around the clock. The telephone number is: +41 22 331 41 33.

Local Attractions

The Cultural Corner delivers information about Geneva in general, and sells tickets for cultural activities in the region, such as festivals, concerts, theatre plays, exhibits, etc. It is open from 09:00 to 17:00 and located near door C6.

Visit the official Welcome to Geneva website for additional tourist information about Geneva.
