We look forward to seeing you in March 2020.

We are pleased to announce that the Global Methane Forum 2020 (GMF 2020) will be held in Geneva, Switzerland from 23‑27 March 2020.

  • The theme of GMF 2020 is Taking Action for Sustainable Energy and a Clean Environment.
  • GMF 2020 will also feature a recognition celebration for participants in the Global Methane Challenge.
