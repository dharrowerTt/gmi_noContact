International Finance Panel: Different Approaches to Funding Methane Projects

Moderator: Laurence Blandford, Environment Canada

Recognizing the important role the financial sector will play in the post-Kyoto world, this plenary session will look at a case study and explore the necessary factors needed to finance a project. This Town Hall-format session will provide an opportunity to hear from key players engaged in the discussion on global methane finance.
