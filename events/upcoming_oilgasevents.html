A Call to Action on Methane: an international dialogue hosted by the GMI

3 June 2021, Microsoft Live Event

Register today for A Call to Action on Methane: an international dialogue hosted by the Global Methane Initiative (GMI) on Thursday, 3 June 2021 from 8:00–10:30 am ETD (UTC -4). This event will use the Microsoft Live platform

The event aims to raise international awareness of the critical need to reduce methane emissions, emphasize opportunities for international leaders in industry, private sector, and government to take action to reduce methane, and celebrate the recharter of the Global Methane Initiative for another 10 years.

Join distinguished members of the methane mitigation community in discussions regarding methane mitigation policies, global trends in the energy sector, and opportunities for action on methane. The event will also feature an industry panel discussion with experts in the Biogas, Oil & Gas, and Coal Mines sectors. A preliminary agenda is available on the event website.
